## 🔴 BREAKING / TOP STORIES

- **WEMBANYAMA SIGNS 5-YEAR, $252M SUPERMAX EXTENSION WITH SPURS.** Victor Wembanyama's rookie-scale max extension became official July 10, locking the reigning Defensive Player of the Year in San Antonio through 2031-32. Notably, he took the 25% max ($252M) rather than pushing for the 30% supermax escalators that could have reached ~$303M — leaving money on the table to give the Spurs flexibility.
  - *Why it matters:* The franchise cornerstone is committed long-term, and the "Jalen Brunson blueprint" (take slightly less, help build a winner) is now the young-star trend. Huge for San Antonio's timeline.

- **FOUR-TEAM LaMELO BALL BLOCKBUSTER MADE OFFICIAL.** The Timberwolves-Hornets-Nets-Bulls deal cleared July 10: Minnesota lands **LaMelo Ball** (+ Josh Green + rights to Isaiah Evans); Charlotte gets **Naz Reid** + Mouhamadou Gueye + a haul of picks/swaps (incl. Wolves' 2033 1st); Brooklyn gets **Julius Randle** + rights to Joshua Jefferson; Chicago gets **Nic Claxton**.
  - *Why it matters:* Minnesota is going all-in — pairing LaMelo with Anthony Edwards is one of the most electric young backcourts in the league. Charlotte fully commits to a rebuild and stockpiles draft capital.

- **KAWHI LEONARD TRADE TO RAPTORS ON HOLD — ASPIRATION PROBE.** Toronto and the Clippers paused the agreed Kawhi deal pending the NBA's investigation into a $28M endorsement contract between Leonard and the now-bankrupt firm Aspiration (cap-circumvention question). No timetable; outside counsel Wachtell Lipton is running the probe. The trade is paused, *not* dead.
  - *Why it matters:* A star swap frozen by a league investigation is unprecedented drama. If penalties land, it could reshape the return package — or blow the deal up entirely.

- **LEBRON STILL UNDECIDED — RICH PAUL: "THERE IS NONE. I WISH I KNEW."** Asked July 10 for an update, LeBron's agent said flatly there's no news and "he's really evaluating things." Cavs, Heat and 76ers are the frontrunners, with Minnesota, Denver and Golden State on the periphery. His extra time in Akron fuels the Cleveland-homecoming narrative.
  - *Why it matters:* Multiple rosters are frozen waiting on him. The saga now stretches past two weeks — pure content fuel.
