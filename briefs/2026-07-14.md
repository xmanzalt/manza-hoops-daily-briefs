- **LeBRON DECISION WATCH HITS FEVER PITCH — ALL EYES ON FANATICS FEST.** Still no signing as of July 13, but the speculation exploded: LeBron is booked to appear at **Fanatics Fest in NYC (Javits Center, July 16–19)**, recording his *Mind the Game* podcast **July 16–17 with Tyrese Haliburton** — and the internet is convinced the announcement could drop on that stage. - **LAKERS SIGN ZIAIRE WILLIAMS (1YR / $3M).** LA's first move of the post-LeBron era: free-agent wing **Ziaire Williams** agreed to a one-year, $3M deal (Shams/ESPN). The California native wanted to be home and in a "winning situation."
  - *Why it matters:* A cheap, athletic 3-and-D wing on a one-year flier — the Lakers filling out the roster around Luka Dončić while their cap picture stays flexible.

- **FRANK VOGEL JOINS THE WARRIORS BENCH.** Former Lakers/Pacers/Suns head coach **Frank Vogel is joining Steve Kerr's staff in Golden State** (Marc Stein).
  - *Why it matters:* A championship head coach as a lead assistant is a serious defensive upgrade for a Warriors team retooling around Curry, Butler and Horford.
